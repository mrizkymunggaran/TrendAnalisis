REPUBLIKA.CO.ID, JAKARTA - Indonesian President Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono (SBY) supports students of senior high schools who will undergo final exam on Monday on his newly-made Twitter account, @SBYudhoyono. \Happy Final Exam for students. Ibu Ani and me wish you good luck *SBY*,\" tweeted SBY on Sunday. The tweet is marked with *SBY*, which shows that the tweet was made originally by the president. SBY also reminded parents to motivate and pray for their children. He also gave suggestion to parents. \"Don`t put too much pressure *SBY*,\" he said in Twitter.    "
